About Us

Welcome to Hotel Tarangan is a leading provider of hotel accommodation worldwide, offering booking services through its own network of localised websites. Hotels.com gives travellers one of the widest selections of accommodation on the net, including both independent and major chain hotels as well as self-catering in over hundreds of thousands properties worldwide.

The company offers a one-stop shopping source for hotel pricing, amenities and availability. Hotel Tarangan, L.P. is part of the Expedia Group of travel companies. Hotel Tarangan, L.P. headquarters are located at Niphad in Nashik district.
